If anyone’s safety is at risk, ensure safety first and contact local authorities if needed, then report in the app.

Matching & Discover

How do I start meeting people?

Browse lookbooks in Discover, then pass or like. Mutual likes create a match so you can chat in Messages.

What do preferences affect?

Under Profile → Preferences, adjust who you’d like to meet and your location scope for recommendations.

What happens when I block someone?

They can’t match or message you and won’t appear in Discover. Blocking is not notified to the other person.

Glow Points

What are Glow Points for?

Activity commitment fees for hosting or joining offline meetups. Virtual in-app currency — not redeemable for cash.

How do I top up?

Profile → My Points or Top up, then purchase via App Store / Google Play.

Refunds when cancelling an activity?

Host cancel: participants refunded per rules. Self-cancel: full base fee if more than 24h before start; early-bird unlock fee non-refundable; no refund within 24h. See Terms of Use.

Activities

How do I join or host?

Open the Activities tab to browse or create a meetup. When hosting, set the start time at least two days (48 hours) ahead so others have time to see and join; the detailed meetup info is revealed to registered participants 24 hours before it starts. You must accept the safety rules before your first join.

Host no-show?

After the event starts, registered participants can report a no-show from activity details. Points may auto-refund if thresholds are met.

Reporting activity issues?

Use the menu on activity details when registered. You may withdraw and resubmit pending reports, up to two times per report type.

Account

Forgot password or can’t sign in?

Email users: Profile → Account & Security → send reset email. Google / Apple: manage credentials on those platforms.

How do I delete my account?

Profile → “Delete account and remove my data” at the bottom. This permanently deletes your profile, matches, and unused points.

Notification settings?

Profile → Notification Settings to toggle match, message, activity, and points alerts.
