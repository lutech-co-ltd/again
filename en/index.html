The App

Four tabs, one gentle path

  1. Discover

    Browse member lookbooks one at a time. Pass, save for later, or like; you may see a gentle compatibility note. Mutual likes create a match. Adjust preferences and location scope anytime.

  2. Messages

    Chat after you match. Block or report inappropriate behavior to protect your boundaries.

  3. Activities

    Host or join offline social meetups, including family-friendly options. Mood tags, icebreaker warm-ups, and gathering-fit notes help set the tone; optional post-event light connect after meetups. Glow Points serve as activity commitment fees; safety rules apply before joining.

  4. Profile

    Edit your lookbook, set gathering & social preferences, top up points, manage notifications, legal & privacy, account security, and help.

What Makes AGAIN Different

Designed for Mature Relationships

  1. 01

    Story Lookbook

    Introduce yourself through stories, not labels — your profile reads like a personal lookbook.

  2. 02

    Compatibility Notes

    Values, lifestyle, and gathering style inform gentle hints and discover notes — for feeling, not scoring or guarantees.

  3. 03

    Slow Discovery

    No endless scrolling — focus on one person at a time. “Save for later” lets you pause without notifying them.

  4. 04

    Offline Meetups

    Move from online connection to real gatherings. Hosts can pick icebreaker questions; attendees may add warm-up replies. Within 72 hours after an event, mutual light connect opens a timed chat.

  5. 05

    Glow Points

    In-app virtual currency for joining or hosting activities. Top up via App Store / Google Play; not redeemable for cash.

  6. 06

    Safe Community

    Report, block, and review workflows including activity reports and host no-show handling. Activity trust badges reflect attendance records, not identity verification. See our Community Guidelines.

  7. 07

    Post-Event Light Connect

    After meeting offline, if both of you want to stay in touch, open a time-limited chat with a message cap. One-sided choices stay private — you set the pace.

Community Safety

Report or block from Discover, chat, matched profiles, or activity details. In emergencies, contact local authorities first, then support@again.app.
